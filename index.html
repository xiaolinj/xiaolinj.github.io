About Me

Hello! I'm Xiaolin Jiang, a Ph.D. graduate from the University of California, Riverside, focusing on efficient graph query evaluation and large-scale data processing. I am now working at Google as a Software Engineer on the F1 query engine team. In past two years, I worked in Global Network Team. I am open to new research opportunities and also serve as a paper reviewer.
